NotesDate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

NotesDate ("the App") is a personal notes app developed for Android and iOS. This page explains what information the App accesses on your device and, most importantly, what it does not do with it.

1. No Account, No Server

NotesDate does not require you to create an account, sign in, or connect to the internet to use it. The App has no backend server. Your notes, titles, moods, colors, and settings are stored only in the App's local storage on your own device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Your notes' content, titles, or attachments
  • Your name, email, phone number, or any account identifiers
  • Your location
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or advertising identifiers
  • Biometric data (see Section 4)

3. Local Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions. Each is used only for the feature described, entirely on-device:

  • Notifications — used only for the optional "Time Capsule" feature, to remind you when a note you scheduled becomes unlocked. Notifications are generated and scheduled locally on your device; nothing is sent to any server.
  • Fingerprint / Face ID / Screen Lock — used only for the optional "Privacy Lock" feature on individual notes. Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android/iOS). The App never receives, stores, or has access to your actual fingerprint or face data — it only receives a yes/no result from the OS.
  • Storage / File access — used only when you choose to use the "Backup" or "Restore" feature, to save or read a backup file that you explicitly choose to create, share, or open. The App does not access your files at any other time.

4. Backup & Sharing

If you use the Backup feature, the App creates a file containing your notes and settings, and hands it to your device's native share sheet. You decide where that file goes (e.g., Google Drive, Files app, or another app) — the App itself does not upload it anywhere. Likewise, the Share/Copy buttons on individual notes only pass text to apps or clipboards that you choose.

5. Advertising

NotesDate does not display advertisements and does not use any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

NotesDate does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all. The App is a general-audience productivity tool.

7. Data Deletion

Because all data lives only on your device, you can delete everything at any time by deleting your notes in the App, or by uninstalling the App entirely (which removes all locally stored data along with it).

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a revised "Last updated" date.
